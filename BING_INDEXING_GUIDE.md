## Common Reasons Bing Doesn't Crawl

1. **New Site** - Bing may take 1-4 weeks to crawl new sites
2. **Low Authority** - New domains need time to build trust
3. **No Backlinks** - Sites with no external links crawl slower
4. **JavaScript-Heavy** - Ensure content is server-rendered or accessible
5. **Server Issues** - Ensure site is always accessible
